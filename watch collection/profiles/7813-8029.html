Aron's Watch Collection - Seiko 7813-8029

Brand : Seiko

Movement Type : Quartz

Description : This is a 14k gold plated Seiko with an English & Arabic date feature.

Story : This was one of my first watches that I bought to repair and it has been quite problematic. When I first bought it, I couldn't get it open, so I had to bring it to a jewellery store (who failed) and then finally to a watch maker. After that, I replaced the battery, repaired the glass and bought a brand new leather band. This was my first successful glass repair, so it was a proud accomplishment for me. A few days after replacing the glass however, it started randomly going backwards in time. I fixed this by installing a better new battery and it works perfectly. It is currently my favorite watch in my collection because I love how it looks and because of the work that went into it.
