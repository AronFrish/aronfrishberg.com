New York teen mentored by world-renowed hedge fund manager

Aron Frishberg, a driven teenager hailing from New York, had the extraordinary privilege of being personally mentored by Bill Ackman, the globally acclaimed hedge fund manager and CEO of Pershing Square Capital. With Ackman's remarkable presence on the Forbes 400 list and his management of one of the world's largest hedge funds, boasting an impressive $18.5 billion in assets under management (AUM), this mentorship opportunity proved to be a transformative experience for young Aron.
