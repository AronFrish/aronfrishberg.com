Envrionmental Art Project

Many experts believe that by 2100, as much as 23% of New York City will be under the sea level, with much more of the city becoming victim to extreme costal flooding. This is nothing short of a catastrophe. In wake of this new research, I've collaborated with David Zoota, a New York based photographer, to spread the word. I created a complex program using Python that modifies his photographs to represent this scary statistic by modifying 23% of each photograph. Over the past few months, I have been selling this art, printed out on environmentally friendly poster paper, with the entirety of their proceeds going to Environmental Advocates NY, a charity dedicated to fighting Climate Change in New York.
